• To bring equal access to quality to education by providing equitable platform for the underprivileged and vulnerable students to access quality education
• To bring underprivileged students who are unable to secure their own private study classes and avail to them equal opportunities in accessing quality education.
• To recognize the efforts of teachers in national development
• To identify potential students through examination based requirements and sponsor them to write private WASSCE examination from an early stage.


• To provide scholarship to vulnerable students to further their tertiary education through fund raising partnership and or sponsorship.
• To protect and enhance human values in order to raise self-esteem and maintain moral standards among students.
• To foster mutual understanding and cordial relationship among students.
• To embark on meaningful developmental activities in school and among students of the foundation.
• To provide students with internship programs upon finishing high school while awaiting for their results.
• To provide a platform for mentorship and ethical leadership development
